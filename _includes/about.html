Welcome to TOP POLYMERS

Top Polymers is one of the leading indenting company in Bangladesh. It’s sister concern of Top Rank was formed in the year 1987. Since then, the company has been engaged in trading of Polymer, PVC Chemicals, Ceramic Raw Materials, Glass Raw Materials, Film, Oleo Chemicals, Pulp & Paper, Solvent, Synthetic Rubber, etc. The company was established by two individuals who are blessed with many years’ experience in the Trading and import/export arena.

Mission

To develop and maintain long-term business relationship with our suppliers and buyers by creating a win-win situation.

Commitment

Uncompromising integrity.

Strengthen supply chain of our customers and suppliers.

To supply quality materials.

Future Opportunities

We are aggressively seeking to represent foreign (non –Bangladeshi) Industries or suppliers for Bangladesh market for Polymer, PVC Chemicals, Ceramic Raw Materials, Glass Raw Materials, Film, Oleo Chemicals, Pulp & Paper, Solvent, Synthetic Rubber etc. With our vast knowledge and contacts in the local and ASEAN market, we would be able to be a good strategic partner for foreign suppliers.
